What will you get here..

This project is like an e- bookstore website where books can be bought from the comfort of home through the Internet. An online bookstore is a virtual store on the Internet where customers can browse the catalog and select books of interest. User can select many books and those books stored in cart. At checkout time, the items in the shopping cart will be presented as an order. At that time, more information will be needed to complete the transaction.
